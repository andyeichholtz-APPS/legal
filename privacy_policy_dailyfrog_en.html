🐸 Privacy Policy – DailyFrog

Last updated: June 2026

Developer: Randy Apps
Contact: andyeichholtz@gmail.com

1. Overview

DailyFrog is a habit tracker app that helps you set daily goals and track your habits. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your daily goals and frog-task markings
  • Habit entries (sleep, exercise, weight, water, meditation)
  • Streak data and statistics
  • App settings (notification times, vacation mode)
  • Motivational quotes history

This data remains on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

DailyFrog displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Advertising ID of your device (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)

This data is used to display personalized advertisements. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can permanently remove ads through the in-app purchase "Ad-free" (one-time fee of €1.49).

4. In-App Purchase

The "Ad-free" purchase is processed through Google Play Billing. Randy Apps does not have access to your payment information. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Google.

5. Notifications

DailyFrog sends push notifications (morning and evening reminders) only if you enable them in the settings. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or in your Android system settings.

6. No Accounts, No Login

DailyFrog does not require any registration or login. No personal data such as name, email address, or passwords is collected.

7. No Third-Party Sharing

Randy Apps does not share any of your data with third parties, except for the technical data arising from AdMob and Google Play Billing (see sections 3 and 4).

8. Children

DailyFrog is intended for users aged 18 and older and is not directed at children under the age of 13.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request information about your stored data at any time.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data.
  • Objection: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Android settings.

For requests, contact: andyeichholtz@gmail.com

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. The current version will always be available at this URL.

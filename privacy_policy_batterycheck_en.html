2. Our Commitment

BatteryCheck was built with privacy in mind. The app works entirely offline. No personal data is transmitted to Randy Apps.

3. What Data Is Collected?

3.1 Data stored locally on your device

  • Battery level, health, temperature and voltage (local only, for the history tab)
  • App settings (temperature unit, notification thresholds, etc.)
  • App open count (for review prompt timing and interstitial ad frequency)
  • In-app purchase status ("Ad-free")

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to Randy Apps.

3.2 Data collected by third parties

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

The app uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by Google. Randy Apps does not receive any payment data.

4. Permissions

  • BATTERY_STATS – To read battery data (health, temperature, voltage)
  • INTERNET – Solely for displaying ads (AdMob)
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – Checks network availability for AdMob
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – For battery warnings (only if permitted by user)
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – For widget updates after device restart

The app does not use camera, microphone, location, contacts, or file storage.

5. Data Sharing

Randy Apps does not share any personal data with third parties. Data collected by third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Play Billing) is subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All locally stored data (history, settings) is automatically purged after 30 days. You can delete all app data at any time via Android system settings: Settings → Apps → BatteryCheck → Storage → Clear Data.

7. Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
